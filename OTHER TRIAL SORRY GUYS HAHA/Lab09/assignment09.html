What are KitKats?

KitKats are a fun treat to share with friends, family, or even a stranger you meet at a party. Want to offer your new friend a piece of chocolate over the table? Snap off one of the fingers from your KitKat and hand it to them; no messy ripping apart of candy bars needed. It’s perfect for when you want a little crunch in your chocolate without the mess of having to bite through a candy bar that’ll leave caramel trails all over the wrapper, and it’s the perfect size for a quick one-handed snack on the way to the bus stop or between classes. If chocolate isn’t for you, you can find other flavours. No matter what your taste in wafer-based snacks is, you’ll always find the perfect snack in a KitKat.

What is Pocky?

Pocky is about sharing happiness with friends, family, co-workers—anyone, anytime, anywhere! Just open a box, pass it around, and watch the smiles and happiness spread. With plenty of sticks in each box, Pocky is the perfect snack for bringing people closer together and livening the mood. Great to have around the house. With the no-mess handle on every Pocky stick, your kids won’t have chocolate all over their hands. And for the same reason, Pocky is also great for parties. Try it as a drink stirrer in a cocktail or in your morning cup of coffee or tea. Bring some Pocky to school or work, and you’ll have a delicious snack to keep you going throughout the day.
